Why Club Maul?

Maul's Mission

Club maul is a virtual scare house experience that aims to take advantage of the VR medium to achieve things that cannot be done IRL, we focus on the scare and hunt experience above all else.

Our events are 18+ only, due to the graphic nature of the hunts and our rules running on a "nude but not lewd" basis.

We alongside our hunt events host hang out / club / game night style events often inviting those even not apart of the club to join us in order to show the community we have built to others.

Our leadership includes a wide variety of experiences that we bring towards our events including work ranging from more strict corporate and military style backgrounds to more open and creative art backgrounds in theater, music and other creative works.

We aim to expand the medium as much as we can, taking advantage of every opportunity vr gives us to enhance every hunt and bring memorable experiences that can't be had anywhere else.

2025 Club Maul — Made with curiosity and teamwork.
